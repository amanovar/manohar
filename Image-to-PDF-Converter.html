Introduction

The Image to PDF Converter simplifies converting images into the PDF format. Users often need to combine notes, assignments, ID proofs, or document photos into one file. This application accepts common image formats like JPG and PNG, then produces a clean, high-quality PDF output. The goal is to make document management faster and easier for students, teachers, and professionals.
